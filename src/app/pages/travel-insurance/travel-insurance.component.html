Business Travel Insurance for GE India, Simplified for you!

Changes are effective for travel booked on or after August 21, 2017

All GE business travelers who are employed in India will follow a new business travel Insurance process when booking international business travel on or after August 21, 2017.A new program through ICICI Lombard will reduce the need for trip-specific insurance certificates. With our new insurance provider ICICI Lombard, your Business Travel Insurance is provided on a blanket basis, meaning that all GE India employees traveling on Company business outside of India are automatically covered by the policy. Personalized travel certificates indicating travel dates and pre-notification of travel plans are no longer necessary to ensure GE Business Travel Insurance coverage for employees of GE India. This approach aligns with the GE Global Business Travel Insurance program used for GE employees of all other countries. It may still be necessary for you to provide an insurance certificate to satisfy requirements to obtain a travel visa for some countries. If necessary, click the link below. You will be directed to the ICICI Lombard website to request a personalized insurance certificate that may be used as proof of coverage
